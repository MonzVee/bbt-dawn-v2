# BBT Weekly Performance Report

Run every Monday morning.

## Instructions
Query Shopify for the past 7 days and report:

1. REVENUE SUMMARY
   - Total revenue this week vs last week
   - Revenue by collection (batteries/tyres/combos/accessories/oils)
   - Online vs in-store split if available
   - Average order value this week vs last week

2. TOP PERFORMERS
   - Top 5 products by revenue
   - Top 5 products by units sold
   - Top 5 products by GP rand value (use GP floors from OPERATIONS.md)

3. UNDERPERFORMERS
   - Bottom 5 products by revenue
   - Any product with zero sales this week
   - Slow movers to consider for flash sale

4. CUSTOMER INSIGHTS
   - New vs returning customers
   - Most common order value range
   - Geographic spread if available

5. FLASH SALE RECOMMENDATION
   - Recommend this week's Friday flash sale product
   - Reasoning: margin, stock level, TikTok potential
   - Suggested sale price and compare-at price
   - Draft broadcast message (3 versions)

6. ACTION ITEMS
   - Top 5 priorities for this week
   - Any supplier reorders needed
   - Content recommendations based on top sellers

Format as a structured weekly report.
Be specific with numbers. Compare to previous week always.
